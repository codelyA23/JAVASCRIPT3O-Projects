About The Challenge

This is my journey through the JavaScript30 challenge created by Wes Bos. The goal is to build 30 things in 30 days using only vanilla JavaScript—no frameworks, no libraries, just pure code.

Each project in this portfolio represents a step forward in mastering the DOM, modern APIs, and creative coding. I'm documenting my process, optimizations, and lessons learned along the way.
